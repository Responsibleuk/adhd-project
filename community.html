About us

People with ADHD can easily be misunderstood and often become isolated from their community lacking the confidence to deal with everyday tasks. However with the right diagnosis, treatment and support ADHD can be a superpower. Welcome to our community for adults with ADHD.

Our strengths may be someone else’s weaknesses and this is beauty of being part of a community. Being part of a shared space, whether physical or virtual, gives people the chance to be inspired, solve problems, share humour, vent their frustrations and share their achievements. People who are part of a community and agree passionately with its values and shared beliefs can lead happier, healthier lives.
